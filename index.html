How AquaPonic Oasis Works

The Aquaponic Cycle

  1. Fish produce nutrient-rich waste
  2. Water is pumped to the plant beds
  3. Plants filter and clean the water
  4. Clean water is returned to the fish tank

This sustainable cycle creates a perfect ecosystem for both plants and fish, producing fresh vegetables and fish for your office community.
